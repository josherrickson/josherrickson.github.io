Chapter 14 # Exercise A3 – Methodology Choice Practice

In the below questions first determine what the appropriate analysis method is based on the variables of interest and carry out these methods within SPSS.

A. From ExerciseA3_Data_A

  1. Is there a relationship between sex (gender) and job category (jobcat)?

  2. Is there a relationship between job category (jobcat) and minority status (minority)?

  3. Is there a relationship between job category (jobcat) and salary (salary)?

  4. Is there a relationship between experience (jobtime) and salary (salary)?

B. From ExerciseA3_Data_B

  1. Is there a relationship between general happiness (happy) and occupational prestige score (prestg80)?

  2. Is there a relationship between age (age) and occupational prestige score (prestg80)?

  3. Is there a relationship between general happiness (happy) and perception of life being exciting or dull (life)?
